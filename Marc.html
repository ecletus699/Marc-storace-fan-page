About Marc Storace

Marc Storace is a renowned Swiss rock singer, best known as the lead vocalist of the iconic hard rock band Krokus. With his powerful voice and dynamic stage presence, Marc has captured the hearts of fans worldwide since the 1980s.

Born in Malta, Marc's early influences came from classic rock bands, which shaped his musical style. He joined Krokus in the 1980s, and together, they quickly became one of Europe's most celebrated rock acts. Marc's distinctive voice and charismatic performances were key to the band's success, especially during their most successful albums such as Headhunter and The Blitz.

Beyond his work with Krokus, Marc has also pursued a solo career, showcasing his versatility as an artist. His passion for music is evident not only in his powerful vocal performances but also in his commitment to connecting with his fans on a personal level.

Throughout his career, Marc has toured extensively, performing at some of the biggest rock festivals and arenas around the world. With a legacy of timeless rock anthems, Marc continues to inspire and entertain fans both old and new, solidifying his place in the pantheon of rock legends.
